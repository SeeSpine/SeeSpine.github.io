Frequently Asked Questions

If you are still growing:
a) Curves less than 20°: observation via X-rays every 6 months.
b) Curves between 20° to 50°: continued X-ray monitoring and a back brace-- different types of braces are available depending on the individual curve pattern.
c) Curves greater than 50°: surgery is indicated.
If you are finished growing:
a) Curves less than 50°: no treatment.
b) Curves greater than 50°: surgery.
